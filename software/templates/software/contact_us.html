Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download software from your platform?
Simply browse our software catalog, click on any software you're interested in, and click the download button. No registration required for downloading free software.
Is all software on your platform free?
Yes, we focus on free and open-source software. All software available for download is free to use, though some may have specific license terms.
How do I report a bug or security issue?
Please use the contact form above with "Bug Report" as the subject, or email us directly at support@softwareportal.com with detailed information about the issue.
Can I upload my own software to the platform?
Yes! Create an account and use our upload feature. All software goes through a review process to ensure quality and security before being published.
How do you ensure software safety?
We scan all uploaded software for malware, review source code when available, and maintain strict quality guidelines. However, we recommend using antivirus software as an additional precaution.
